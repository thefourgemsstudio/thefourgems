The Four Gems — Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how The Four Gems Studio ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use The Four Gems mobile game.

Effective date: 2025-09-28

1. Information we collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide and improve the game. Data types may include:

  • Gameplay data — levels completed, scores, progress, in-game purchases.
  • Device & technical data — device model, OS version, unique device identifiers, crash logs.
  • Analytics & performance — aggregated usage statistics to help us understand and improve the game experience.
  • Advertising identifiers — advertising ID (for personalized ads if you opt-in).

2. How we use your information

  • To operate, maintain and improve the game.
  • To process purchases and manage subscriptions.
  • To provide targeted or non-targeted ads (depending on your consent).
  • To detect and prevent fraud and abuse.

3. Third-party services

We use trusted third-party services to help run The Four Gems. These may include:

  • Google Play Services (distribution, billing) — Privacy Policy
  • AdMob (ads) — Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Games Services (cloud save, achievements) — Privacy Policy

Each third party has its own privacy policy and data practices. We recommend reviewing their policies for details.

4. Ads and tracking choices

If you are located in the EU/EEA or UK, we will ask for consent before showing personalized ads. You can change ad preferences in the game settings or by clearing your device advertising identifier.

5. In-app purchases & payments

We do not handle payment card information directly. Purchases and billing are processed via the app store billing providers (e.g., Google Play Billing).

6. Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the services, comply with legal obligations, and improve the game. Aggregate or anonymized data may be kept longer for analytics purposes.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us (details below).

8. Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect user data. However, no system is completely secure — use caution when sharing sensitive information.

9. Minors

The Four Gems is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the applicable minimum age without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us to request deletion.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above will reflect the latest version.
